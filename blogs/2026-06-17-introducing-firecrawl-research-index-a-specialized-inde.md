Firecrawl launched a specialized search index for AI/ML research that delivers 53.3% recall at $0.32 per task, versus competitors at 45.4% recall. The index covers all 3M+ arXiv papers plus GitHub artifacts, enabling AI agents to autonomously retrieve papers, verify claims, and access implementation code.
